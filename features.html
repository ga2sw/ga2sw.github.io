Google Analytics to Salesforce Wave

Simple Integration via APIs

Features

  • Google Analytics to Salesforce Wave(GA2SW) uses Google Analytics metrics and dimensions, to dynamically create a Google analytical query suited to your business requirements
  • Has the ability to maintains multiple personas; in this way, business needs would be met from a flexible and a need based criteria at any level
  • Can effectively create cross combination of personas
  • Has the capacity to load and create a dataset from the data extracted from Google Analytics
  • Can meet your business reporting and data interpretation needs by defining and scheduling jobs
