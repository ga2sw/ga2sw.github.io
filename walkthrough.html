Simple Integration via APIs

Walkthrough

1. Manage profile tab : Log into the web application; enter all your personal credentials

2. Manage users : Add, edit, remove and view users who have access to the application

3. Add user: Fill in credentials of the new user and assign role

4. Manage GA Profiles : Add, edit, remove and view your current profiles

5. Add a new website : Fill in credentials of the new site details and press connect under "actions" once added

6. Manage Salesforce Analytics Profiles : Add, edit, remove and view your current profiles

7. Add a new website : Fill in credentials of your chosen SA profile

8. Select "Dashboard" from top menu : View all jobs currently inactive, active or pending

9. Select "add job" : And create a new job with your required metrics and dimensions through a drop-down list

10. Log into your Salesforce account : From top right hand drop-down, select "Analytics"

11. Select your data set : from the personas from the application

12. Select your reporting needs : From measures, groups and charting options

13. Edit your report : to suit your business reporting needs
